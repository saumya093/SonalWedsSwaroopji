How it all began?

  • He Proposed...

    May 9th 2014

    They were in post-graduation together when Swaroopji started liking Sonal, noticing small things about her and finding them cute and loveable. The more time he spent with her, the more he loved her and finally on this day he gathered all the courage and proposed to her.

  • She said yes :D

    October 3rd 2014

    Well, after the proposal Sonal was both surprised and happy. She didn’t answer immediately but took some time and realized that all those times spent with him were special and she too started loving him just that she didn’t realize it then but finally she said yes.

  • He met bride's mother for the first time

    February 26th 2015

    He met Sonal’s mother for the first time and was overwhelmed with love and affection by which he was welcomed in their family.

  • She met groom's mother for the first time

    February 27th 2015

    She met Swaroopji’s mother for the first time and realized that now she has two families who love her unconditionally.

  • They got engaged

    July 24th 2015

    Now, it was the time to take their relationship to the next level and make their bond even stronger, so they got engaged on this day.

  • And finally, they are getting married :)

    November 19th 2016

    They will be celebrating their love for each other on this day by taking each other and uniting two families into a world full of happiness and joy.
